AI Career Guide

Roadmap from student to AI professional

Data Scientist

Extract insights from data and build predictive models to solve business problems.

Statistics Python/R Machine Learning Data Visualization SQL

Learning Roadmap:

Foundation (Months 1-3)

Learn Python, statistics, and basic data analysis with pandas.

Core Skills (Months 4-6)

Master machine learning algorithms and data visualization techniques.

Advanced Topics (Months 7-9)

Deep learning, big data tools, and end-to-end project implementation.

Portfolio & Job Prep (Months 10-12)

Build portfolio projects, prepare for interviews, and apply for positions.

Machine Learning Engineer

Design, build, and deploy machine learning models at scale in production environments.

ML Algorithms Software Engineering Cloud Platforms MLOps Deep Learning

Recommended Courses:

  • Machine Learning Specialization (Coursera)
  • Deep Learning Specialization (deeplearning.ai)
  • ML Engineering for Production (MLOps) Specialization

AI Research Scientist

Conduct original research to advance the field of artificial intelligence.

Advanced Mathematics Research Methodology PyTorch/TensorFlow Academic Writing Latest AI Papers

Future Scope:

AI research scientists are in high demand at tech companies, research labs, and academia. With the rapid growth of AI, opportunities exist in specialized areas like computer vision, NLP, robotics, and reinforcement learning.
